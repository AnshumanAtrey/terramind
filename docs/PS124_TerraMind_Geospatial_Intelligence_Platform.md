# ITM Skills University

## Case Study 124: Project TerraMind – Global Geospatial Intelligence and Earth Observation Platform

---

TerraMind Analytics operates a worldwide geospatial intelligence platform that integrates satellite imagery, drone surveillance systems, environmental sensors, mapping services, weather intelligence feeds, and AI-powered analytics engines. Governments, defense agencies, environmental organizations, agricultural enterprises, and disaster management authorities depend on the platform for mission-critical decision-making. The system continuously processes petabytes of imagery and billions of telemetry events generated by earth observation assets distributed across the globe.

The current environment includes multiple independent processing systems deployed across different cloud environments and research facilities. Increasing demand for near real-time geospatial analytics has exposed weaknesses in infrastructure scalability, deployment automation, monitoring, and disaster recovery. Several operational incidents involving delayed image processing, storage failures, cloud outages, and cybersecurity threats disrupted critical intelligence workflows. Stakeholders require strict availability targets, operational continuity, and rapid recovery capabilities.

Executive leadership has approved a cloud-native modernization initiative focused on automation, resilience, observability, and operational efficiency. Infrastructure must be provisioned through Terraform, applications containerized using Docker, deployments managed through Kubernetes, and CI/CD pipelines implemented through Jenkins. Monitoring and observability must be implemented through Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack, and Vault. During final evaluation, reviewers may simulate satellite communication failures, storage corruption incidents, cyberattacks, cloud-region outages, analytics pipeline failures, and massive increases in imagery processing workloads.

**Objective:** The objective of this project is to design, implement, and demonstrate a cloud-native DevOps ecosystem supporting geospatial intelligence operations, infrastructure automation, operational monitoring, and disaster recovery.

**Expected Outcome and Deliverables:** Students are expected to deliver infrastructure automation, Dockerized services, Kubernetes deployments, Jenkins CI/CD pipelines, monitoring systems, centralized logging, Vault integration, disaster recovery procedures, security controls, and architecture documentation demonstrating resilience and operational excellence.
